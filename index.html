Medical Logic & Connection

● Non-linear Dynamics & Biological Rhythms:
The instability and exponential growth in the Lato Lato system share high similarity with human "Arrhythmia." Cardiac pacing signals are essentially non-linear oscillatory systems; understanding how parameter perturbations lead to chaos can help predict cardiac failure risks.

● Necessity of Precision Medicine:
Since complex systems are so sensitive to initial conditions, clinical diagnosis cannot rely solely on "population averages." This reinforces my passion for Precision Medicine—treating every patient as a unique set of parameters.
